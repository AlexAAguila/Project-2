We Love Cats

Located in the heart of Coquitlam, The Cat Inn is only about a 10 minute drive from the nearest Skytrain Station, Coquitlam Centre. The Cat Inn offers multiple options for its guests including private rooms, rooms with windows, grooming services, pickups/dropoffs and unlimited attention when needed! Dining options are available and include: wet food, dry food (and a lot of treats).
